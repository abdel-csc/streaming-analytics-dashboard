Retention & Churn Analytics

Subscriber lifecycle modeling, churn prediction, and engagement segmentation

Peacock Subscriber Growth & Estimated Churn Rate

Monthly Retention Curve by Cohort

Analyst note: Peacock grew from 7M to 46M subscribers between Q1 2021 and mid-2025. Growth spikes are driven by tentpole live events (Olympics, NFL). The key strategic challenge is converting event-driven subscribers into retained long-term users. Industry benchmarks suggest streaming services average 5-8% monthly churn; Peacock's ad-supported tier likely trends toward the higher end given its freemium conversion dynamics.
